# Code of Conduct

KnowMesh is an open-source project for building auditable knowledge-base systems. We want project discussion to stay useful, direct, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Discuss ideas, code, design tradeoffs, and evidence rather than personal attributes.
- Assume local documents, credentials, logs, and generated artifacts may be sensitive.
- Redact secrets, private source text, and private paths before sharing issues or logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information, credentials, or source documents.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion after maintainers ask to return to topic.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its users. Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
